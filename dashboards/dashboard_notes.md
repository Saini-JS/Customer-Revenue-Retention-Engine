# Dashboard Notes

This folder contains screenshots of the final Tableau outputs used in the project.

---

## Dashboard 1 – CFO Lens
Purpose:
- show profit leakage
- highlight revenue at risk and revenue already lost
- reveal where value is concentrated
- identify where complaint-linked loss is most visible

Audience:
- CFO
- commercial leadership
- senior stakeholders interested in margin, leakage, and financial exposure

Core themes:
- financial performance
- value concentration
- regional leakage
- complaint-linked exposure
- commercial recovery context

---

## Dashboard 2 – Growth & Retention Engine
Purpose:
- prioritise retention action
- identify which customers require intervention
- show how service quality links to churn risk
- support operational decision-making

Audience:
- Head of Operations
- Customer / Retention leadership
- operational decision-makers

Core themes:
- customer prioritisation
- service-risk signals
- intervention workflow
- action-oriented retention management

---

## Notes
The dashboards were intentionally designed for two different decision layers:

- Dashboard 1 = executive financial lens
- Dashboard 2 = operational action lens

This separation reflects how analytics products are often used in real business settings: one layer for leadership visibility, and one layer for operational execution.
